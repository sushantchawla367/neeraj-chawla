SERVICES

How I Can Support You

Comprehensive counseling services designed to meet you where you are and guide you towards where you want to be.

Individual Counseling

One-on-one therapy sessions tailored to your unique needs and goals. We work together to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors in a safe, supportive environment.

✓ Personalized treatment approach
✓ Confidential and non-judgmental
✓ Flexible session scheduling
✓ Progress tracking and goal setting

Anxiety Support

Specialized support for managing and overcoming anxiety. Learn evidence-based techniques to understand your anxiety triggers and develop effective coping strategies.

✓ Understanding anxiety patterns
✓ Breathing and relaxation techniques
✓ Cognitive restructuring
✓ Building resilience

Behavioral Adjustments

Practical guidance for making positive changes in your life. Whether it's breaking unhelpful habits or building new healthy patterns, I'm here to support your transformation.

✓ Habit formation strategies
✓ Motivation and accountability
✓ Practical action plans
✓ Ongoing support and adjustments

Supporting All Age Groups

My services are available to individuals at every stage of life, from adolescents navigating school challenges to adults managing life transitions.

Adolescents & Teens

Support for school stress, peer relationships, identity exploration, and emotional regulation.

Adults

Help with work-life balance, relationship issues, anxiety management, and personal development.

Older Adults

Guidance through life transitions, retirement adjustments, grief, and maintaining mental wellness.

Not Sure Which Service Is Right for You?

Book a free 15-minute introductory call. We'll discuss your needs and I'll recommend the best approach for your situation.
