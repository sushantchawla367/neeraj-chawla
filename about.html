My Approach

I take a holistic approach to counseling, recognizing that each person is unique and requires an individualized treatment plan. My practice integrates various evidence-based modalities to best serve your needs.

Whether you're dealing with anxiety, seeking behavioral change, or simply need someone to talk to, I provide a safe, confidential space for exploration and healing.

What to Expect

  • A warm, non-judgmental environment where you feel heard
  • Confidential sessions tailored to your specific needs
  • Practical strategies you can apply in daily life
  • Regular progress reviews and adjusted goals
  • Support between sessions when needed
